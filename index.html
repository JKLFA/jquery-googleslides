jQuery.googleslides

A jQuery plugin to display your Google Photos, including Picasa and Google+ albums.

Usage

  1. Add a reference to jquery.googleslides.css and jquery.googleslides.jss. 
    <link href="src/jquery.googleslides.css"
    rel="stylesheet" type="text/css" /> <script src="src/jquery.googleslides.js"
    type="text/javascript"></script>
  2. Add a <div id='yourDivID' /> element to your body to act as the slides container.
  3. Call $('#yourDivID').picasaSlides({user : '115528839112598673902', albumid: '5708746270943603681'}); in $(document).ready(function()...
